Who We Are

Established in 2012, UniGo Consult has grown quickly into a new kind of management consultancy that provides guidance and unique learning opportunities to a wide range of clients.

We are expanding fast, but remain a deliberately small company committed to providing high-quality support to a select number of clients. We apply our many years’ experience inside the education industry to quietly disrupt the sector from within.

Our partnerships are based on trust, clear communication and confidentiality.

We are also pioneering our fast-growing service of providing original, bespoke syllabus and course content.
